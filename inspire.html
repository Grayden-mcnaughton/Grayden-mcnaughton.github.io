Who is T. L. Barret?

Rev. T.L. Barrett, is an American Pentecostal preacher and gospel musician.

Barrett, recording as Pastor T.L. Barrett and the Youth for Christ Choir, released the album Like a Ship (Without a Sail) in 1971 which became one of his most influencial and popular albums to date.
His music has touched generations, and his faith and detication to the gospel is an inspiration.
